Basket Ball
We won Basket Ball laurels at the 3rd National Student Games 2019-20

The girl sportstars of DAV Public School Pakhowal Road Ludhiana represented Punjab State in the 3rd National Student Games 2019-20 organized by Students Games Federation of India on 20 th and 21st July 2019 at Sujanpur Tihra, Himachal Pradesh. The girls team rocked the national platform with their unsurpassable performance in U-22 Basket Ball and U-14 Badminton Championships. Tonight The U-22 Girls team including Avleen Kaur, Pretty Sidhu, Kanishka, Bhavika, Jasleen, Parneet, Sahaj, Aditi and Deepinder bagged Gold Medals for their excellent performance. The U-14 Badminton players Karul and Anvi bagged Gold and Kashvi grabbed the Silver.

  • About School

    D.A.V. Public School, Sarabha Nagar Ext., Ludhiana came into existence in the year 2004. The school is being run by D.A.V. College Trust and Management Society, founded in 1885 by socially motivated people greatly influenced by the social reforms movement started by Maharishi Dayanand Saraswati. The first D.A.V. Institution was setup in Lahore in 1886 with Mahatama Hansraj as its Headmaster who dedicated his entire life to the cause of education. In the post-Independence times, the great dedication and determination of visionaries like Justice Meharchand Mahajan, Lala Suraj Bhan, Justice Jiwan Lal Kapur, Dr. G.L. Dutta and presently Padam Bhushan Shri Gyan Prakash Chopra has made Dayanand Anglo Vedic Trust and Management Society India’s largest educational body. Today the trust is running more than 700 schools and colleges in India and abroad.
